Hogwarts

Type: Curse
Pronunciation: ah-VAH-dah keh-DAV-rah
Description: Causes instant death to the victim wherever it hits on the body, is accompanied by a flash of green light and a rushing noise; there is no known counter-curse, although there are a number of ways to prevent death by it, such as hitting it with another spell in mid-flight, dodging it, or interrupting the caster.
